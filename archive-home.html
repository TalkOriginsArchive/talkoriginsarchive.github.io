TalkOrigins Archive

Exploring the creation/evolution controversy.

Talk.origins is a Usenet newsgroup devoted to discussion and debate about biological and physical origins.

The TalkOrigins Archive collects articles and essays, many originally appearing in talk.origins, to provide mainstream scientific responses to recurring questions and rebutted claims about evolution, intelligent design, and creationist pseudosciences.
