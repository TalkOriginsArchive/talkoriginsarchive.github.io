Debatte mit Kreationisten: Ted Holden

Ted Holden is talk.origin's most long-lived kook, having been around since around the mid-80's, I believe. Ted is not your average creationist, being more into Velikovsky-style Katastrophismus and some noch bizarre Varianten, but he hates evolution with a passion, for reasons that are unclear. Click here for Teds Sicht auf die menschliche Evolution, which is so weird that, to paraphrase physicist Wolfgang Pauli, not only isn't it right, it isn't even wrong.
