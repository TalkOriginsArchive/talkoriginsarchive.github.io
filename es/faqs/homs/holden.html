Debate con creacionistas: Ted Holden

Ted Holden is talk.origin's most long-lived kook, having been around since around the mid-80's, I believe. Ted is not your average creationist, being more into Velikovsky-style catastrofismo and some variantes aún más extrañas, but he hates evolution with a passion, for reasons that are unclear. Click here for La visión de Ted sobre la evolución humana, which is so weird that, to paraphrase physicist Wolfgang Pauli, not only isn't it right, it isn't even wrong. Ted also has a web page on Neandertales.
