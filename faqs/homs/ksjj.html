Debating Creationists: Karl Crawford

This page contains a number of posts to the talk.origins newsgroup by creationist Karl Crawford (aka ksjj, Mr. C) and myself. Observe and admire the dogged determination with which Karl refuses to examine the evidence that he continually asks for.
  • Apr 9, 1996, JF:
    In response to Karl's repeated requests for evidence for evolution, I suggested a number of hominid fossils.
  • Dec 9, 1996, JF:
    I posted the above evidence again, a number of times, in response to continued claims by Karl that no-one had given him any evidence. He finally responded in email, but did not address the evidence.
  • Mar 20, 1997, JF:
    Karl again made frequent requests for pictures of transitional fossils, so I tried, a number of times, to bring some to his attention.
  • Mar 31, 1997, JF:
    Only after emailing him did Karl respond to my posts (although not to the evidence). This is my reply.
  • Nov 26, 1997, JF:
    Karl continued to ask for evidence, and to ignore it when it was presented. This was the third of a number of responses I made to his requests.
  • Nov 27, 1997, KC:
    Karl finally responded but totally ignored the supplied evidence, changing the subject to other fossils and making snide comments.
  • Dec 1, 1997, JF:
    A response to the previous post, which points out that he has not yet addressed the evidence. No reply.
  • Mar 10, 1998, JF:
    Karl repeated his challenge yet again, I responded to it again, and Karl ignored it again.
In response to Karl's requests, I have presented some of the more complete hominid fossils to him at least ten times, and asked him what they are if they are not transitional. Karl has never addressed this evidence.

In case you're wondering, no, I don't continue this debate with any real expectation that Karl will ever honestly address the evidence. The only reason I bother was perfectly summed up by Steve Henderson in a post on talk.origins:

As everybody on t.o knows, Karl doesn't read well, doesn't do any research beyond finding chunks of material by other creationists to regurgipost, and doesn't understand much of anything about science. Answering his posts would be a waste of time if it weren't that he is such a wonderful example of the basic intellectual worthlessness of creationism.
