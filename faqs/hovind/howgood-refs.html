Notes

* For later Swedish finds, I am indebted to Paul Heinrich who passed along a 1997/10/11 post to talk.origins by Keith Littleton.

† The Roche limit is a distance of 2.44 times a planet's radius, as measured from the center of the planet. Within that distance an orbiting body, if chiefly held together by gravity, will be pulled apart.

‡ A hardground is a distinctive, cemented layer of sedimentary rock, which may be formed when a sea floor remains exposed to the open water for very long periods of time. Hardgrounds often contain little burrows and other structures that are common to aged, permanent sea floors. Contrast that with temporary sea floors due to rapid sedimentation.
