Geologic References on Paluxy Controversy

References on the Geology and Paleontology of the Glen Rose Formation
Glen J. Kuban

[This article is being mirrored from http://paleo.cc/paluxy/georef.htm.]

(Listed alphabetically by author)

Bergan, Gail R. 1988. Shoreline Depositional Environments of Glen Rose Formation (Lower Cretaceous) in Type Area, Somervell and Hood Counties, Texas. American Association of Petroleum Geologists Bulletin 72(9):1109.

Bergan, Gail R., and Jeffrey G. Pittman. 1990. Nearshore Clastic-Carbonate Facies and Dinosaur Trackways in the Glen Rose Formation (Lower Cretaceous) of Central Texas, Geological Society of America Field Trip No. 8, October 27, 1990. Dallas Geological Society, Dallas, Texas. 83 pp. (Dallas Geological Society; One Energy Square, Suite 170; Dallas, Texas 75206).

Bird, Roland T. 1985 (poshumous). Bones for Barnum Brown: Adventures of a Dinosaur Hunter. Texas Christian University Press, Forth Worth, Texas.

Davis, K. W. 1974. Stratigraphy and Depositional Environments of the Glen Rose Formation, North Central Texas. Baylor Geological Studies Bulletin 26. Baylor University, Waco, Texas. 27 pp.

Farlow, James O. 1987a. Lower Cretaceous Dinosaur Tracks, Paluxy River Valley, Texas. Baylor University, Waco, Texas. 110 pp.

Farlow, James O. 1987b. A Guide to Lower Cretaceous Dinosaur Footprints and Tracksites of the Paluxy River Valley, Somervell County, Texas, Geological Society of America , South-Central Section Field Trip, South Central Section Annual Meeting 1987. Geological Society of America, Boulder, Colorado.

Farlow, James O. 1989. Paleobiology of the Dinosaurs Special Paper 238, Geological Society of America, Boulder, Co.

Farlow, James O. 1992. "Of tracks and the River." In W.H. Shore, editor, Mysteries of Life and the Universe: Essays of Americas Finest Writers on Science. pp. 63084. Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, New York.

Frlow, James O., and Michael J. Hawthorne. 1989. Stop 4: Comanchean Dinosaur Footprints. Field Guide to the Vertebrate Paleontology of the Trinity Group, Lower Cretaceous, of Central Texas. Southern Methodist State University, Shuler Museum of Paleontology, Dallas, Texas. pp. 23-30.

Farlow, James O., Jeffrey G. Pittman, and J. Michael Hawthorne. 1989. Brontopodus birdi, Lower Cretaceous Sauropod Footprints from the U.S. Gulf Coastal Plain. Dinosaur Tracks and Traces. Gillette, D. D., and M. G. Lockley, eds. Cambridge University Press, New York, pp. 371-394.

Gillette, D.D. and M. G. Lockley, editors. 1989. Dinosaur Tracks and traces. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, England.

Hawthorne, J. Michael. 1987. The Stratigraphy and Depositional Environments of Lower Cretaceous Dinosaur Track Bearing Strata in Texas. Abstracts With Programs Geological Society of America. 19(3):169-170.

Kuban, G. J. 1989. Elongate Dinosaur Tracks. In Dinosaur Tracks and Traces (D.D. Gillette and M.G. Lockley, Eds.) Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 57-72.

Kuban, G. J. 1989. Color Distinctions and Other Curious Features of Dinosaur Tracks Near Glen Rose, Texas. In Dinosaur Tracks and Traces (D.D. Gillette and M.G. Lockley, Eds.). Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 426-440.

Langston, W., Jr. 1983. Lower Cretaceous Dinosaur Tracks Near Glen Rose, Texas. Lower Cretaceous Shallow Marine Environments in the Glen Rose Formation: Dinosaur Tracks and Plants. Field Guide. B. F. Perkins and W. Langston, Jr., eds. American Association of Stratigraphic Palynologists 12th Annual Meeting, Dallas, Texas. pp. 39-61.

Lockley, M. G. 1991. Tracking Dinosaurs. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, England.

Lockley, M., and Hunt, A., 1995, Dinosaur Footprints and Other Fossil Tracks of the Western United States: Chicago, University of Chicago Press, 285 pp.

Glen J. Kuban, March 1996
