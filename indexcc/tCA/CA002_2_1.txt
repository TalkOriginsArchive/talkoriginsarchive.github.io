BriefClaim: Marx offered to dedicate Das Kapital to Darwin.
Title: Marx dedicating book to Darwin
Keywords: Marx, Darwin, Das Kapital
Obsolete: Merged with CA002_2, which it adds little to, 2004-6-28

Claim:

Marx offered to dedicate _Das Kapital_ to Darwin, and Darwin refused.

Source:

Morris, Henry M. 1973.  Geology and the Flood.  _Impact_ #6.
 http://www.icr.org/pubs/imp/imp-006.htm

Response:

1. This is a myth.  Darwin had a copy of _Das Kapital_, presumably sent by
   Marx, but Darwin never read it.

   Edward Aveling, Marx's son-in-law, sought permission from Darwin to
   dedicate _The Student's Darwin_, an exposition of Darwin's ideas.
   Darwin declined and argued that science should not address religious
   matters directly (Young 1985, 20-21).

2. None of this matters to the science of evolution.
