BriefClaim: Leonardo Da Vinci was a creationist.
Title: Leonardo Da Vinci a creationist.
Keywords: Leonardo Da Vinci, creationism, scientist.

Claim:

Leonardo Da Vinci was a creationist.

Source:

Morris, Henry M. 1982.  Bible-believing scientists of the past.  _Impact_
 103 (Jan.),
 http://www.icr.org/index.php?module=articles&action=view&ID=185

Response:

1. Leonardo Da Vinci's view of science was quite close to today's view.
   He understood the importance of basing science on observing and testing
   the natural world.  He also refused to accept ideas purely on a
   person's authority.

    "Consider now, O reader! what trust can we place in the ancients, who
     tried to define what the Soul and Life are -- which are beyond proof
     -- whereas those things which can at any time be clearly known and
     proved by experience remained for many centuries unknown or falseley
     under-stood (Leonardo 1998, 1).
