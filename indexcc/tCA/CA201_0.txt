Title: Evolution's usefulness
Keywords: theory, fact.

Claim:

Evolution is only a theory.  (A separate page devoted to examples of
evolution's usefulness)

Source:

Response:

1. For a general review, see Bull and Wichman (2001).

2. Phylogenetic analysis is used to predict gene function, which is
   foundational for drug discovery.  (Eisen and Wu 2002; Searls 2003)

3. Phylogenetic techniques are used to track the origins and history of
   pathogens, which has applications in counter-terrorism (Cummings and
   Relman 2002) and vaccine development (Gaschen et al. 2002).

4. Evolutionary theory is used to manage fisheries for greater yields
   (Conover and Munch 2002).
