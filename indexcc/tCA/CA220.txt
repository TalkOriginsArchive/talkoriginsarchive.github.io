BriefClaim: Evolution can not be replicated.
Title: Evolution replicable
Keywords: replication, experiment.

Claim:

Science requires experiments that can be replicated.  Evolution can not be
replicated, so it is not science.

Source:

SciCre, p. 4.

Response:

1. Science requires that observations can be replicated.  The observations
   on which evolution is based, including comparative anatomy, genetics,
   and fossils, are replicable.  In many cases, you can repeat the
   observations yourself.

2. Repeatable experiments, including experiments about mutations and
   natural selection in the laboratory and in the field, also support
   evolution.
