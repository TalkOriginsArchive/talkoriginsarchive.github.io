BriefClaim: Scientists' conclusions are motivated by money.
Title: Scientists' pay.
Keywords: scientist, motivation

Claim:

The conclusions of scientists are motivated by scientists' pay; they
cannot be considered objective.

Source:

Response:

1. Scientists get rewarded for overthrowing currently accepted ideas (if
   they can do so with evidence) and for proposing new theories that lead
   to new research.  Any bias from material gain would be *against* the
   accepted theory of evolution.

2. Many research scientists could make more money in industry.  They do
   science because they enjoy it.

3. The complaint applies equally to anti-evolutionists.
