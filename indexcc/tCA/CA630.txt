BriefClaim: Animals are not moral, aesthetic, idealistic, or religious.
Title: Morals, esthetics, idealism of animals
Keywords: animals, morals

Claim:

Evolution says humans are animals, but humans are moral, esthetic,
idealistic, and religious, and animals are not.

Source:

SciCre, p. 196.

Response:

1. It is not just evolution that says humans are animals.  Any basic
   biology says that.

2. Who says animals are not moral, aesthetic, idealistic, or religious?
   Many animals show altruistic behavior.  Others show respect for their
   dead.  Some writers have claimed, not entirely in jest, that animals
   are more moral than humans.
