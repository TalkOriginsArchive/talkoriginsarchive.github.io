BriefClaim: Do you want to be descended from a monkey?
Title: Monkey's relative
Keywords: monkey, ape, related

Claim:

Evolution says you are descended from a monkey.  Do you want to be
descended from a monkey?

Source:

Moon, Rev. Sun Myung. 1976 (7 Mar.).  The central figure and the
 transitional period.  http://www.unification.net/1976/760307.html

Response:

1. What we want does not determine what is.  To believe that reality will
   bend to our wishes is a form of hubris.

2. What is wrong with being related to a monkey?  In Yoruba tradition, it
   is believed that twins are descended directly from monkeys, and twins
   are considered good fortune (Courlander 1996, 233-238).  Being
   related to a monkey does not make you a monkey.  It does not make you
   any different from what you are.
