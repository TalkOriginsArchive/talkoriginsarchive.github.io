BriefClaim: Why is new life not still being generated today?
Title: Why no abiogenesis today?
Keywords: abiogenesis

Claim:

Abiogenesis assumes life was created by processes still operating today,
so new life should still be appearing today.

Source:

SciCre, pg. 46.


Response:

1. Conditions today are different from conditions in the past in two
   important ways: First, there was little or no molecular oxygen in the
   atmosphere or oceans when life first appeared.  Free oxygen is reactive
   and would likely have interfered with the formation of complex organic
   molecules.  More importantly, there was no life around before life
   appeared.  The life that is around today would scavenge and eat any
   complex molecules before they could turn into anything approaching new
   life.
