BriefClaim: Evolution does not explain art.
Title: Evolution of art
Keywords: art, creativity

Claim:

Evolution does not explain people's desire to create art or their
appreciation of it.

Source:

Response:

1. This is an <CA100>argument from incredulity>.  Not knowing how
   something is explained doesn't mean it can't be explained.

2. Art is a form of creativity, and creativity has obvious evolutionary
   advantages (for example, allowing us to think up new and better tools).
