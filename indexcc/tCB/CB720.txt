Title: Ontogenetic depth
Keywords: ontogenetic depth, development, natural selection

Claim:

"Ontogenetic depth" is "a measure of the distance (in terms of cell
division and differentiation) between a single-celled state and an adult
animal (metazoan) capable of reproduction." (Nelson n.d.)
(Somehow, this is incompatible with natural selection.)
