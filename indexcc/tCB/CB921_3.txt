BriefClaim: Reptile heart to bird heart couldn't happen gradually.
Title: 3- to 4-chambered heart
Keywords: heart, lungfish, reptile, mammal

Claim:

The evolution of the four-chamber mammalian heart from the three-chamber
reptilian heart is implausible because there are no plausible
intermediates.  Simply extending the interventricular septum would block
bloodflow from the atria to the pulmonary trunk, which would kill the
reptile.

Source:

Thomas, Julie, 1992.  More on three and four chamber hearts.
 Message-ID 1992Apr9.050422.21424@usenet.ins.cwru.edu,
 http://www.google.com/groups?as_umsgid=1992Apr9.050422.21424%40usenet.ins.cwru.edu

Partial Response:

1. The mammalian four-chamber heart did not evolve from the three-chamber
   hearts of modern reptiles.  Rather, both evolved from a common
   ancestor.
