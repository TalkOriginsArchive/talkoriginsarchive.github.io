BriefClaim: Evolution does not explain our using one tenth of our brain.
Title: One tenth of the brain
Keywords: brain, selection.

Claim:

According to evolution, we do not evolve organs that are not adaptive.
Therefore, evolution does not explain our using only one tenth of our
brains.

Source:

Response:

1. The belief that we use only ten percent of our brain is an urban legend
   and is false (Radford 2000).  We use all of it.
