BriefClaim: Piltdown man was the subject of 500 doctoral dissertations.
Title: Dissertations on Piltdown
Keywords: Piltdown, hoax, athropology.

Claim:

500 doctoral dissertations were written about Piltdown Man.

Source:

Parker, Gary, 1981. Origin of mankind. _Impact_ 101 (Nov.),
 https://www.icr.org/index.php?module=articles&action=view&ID=183

Response:

1. The claim is false.  In all probability, there were no doctoral
   dissertations on Piltdown Man.  The claim probably arises from an
   editorial in _Nature_ which says, "More than five hundred articles and
   memoirs are said to have been written about Piltdown man."  [Nature
   editors 1954; Harter 1996]

Links:

Harter, Richard, 1996. Piltdown Man:  The bogus bones caper.
 http://www.talkorigins.org/faqs/piltdown.html#doctoral_theses

References:

2. Nature editors, 1954.  The Piltdown bones and 'implements'.  _Nature_
   274(4419): 61-62.
