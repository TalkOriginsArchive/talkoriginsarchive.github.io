BriefClaim: Australopithecus was fully ape, closer to chimp.
Title: Australopithecus an ape?
Keywords: Australopithecus, hominid, bipedalism.

Claim:

_Australopithecus_ was fully ape, closer to chimp.
World-renowned anatomists and evolutionists Solly Zuckerman and Charles
Oxnard have shown that _Australopithecus_ did not walk upright in a human
manner.

Source:

LifeHow, pp.
 93-94.
Yahya, Harun, 2003.  _Darwinism Refuted_, The origin of man.
 https://web.archive.org/web/20200825001720if_/http://www.darwinismrefuted.com/origin_of_man_02.html

Response:

1. _Australopithecus africanus_ and _robustus_ are far more humanlike than
   apelike.  _A. afarensis_ falls somewhere between human and ape, perhaps
   more on the side of ape.  _A. ramidus_ is even more apelike.

   The claims that _africanus_ and _robustus_ are fully ape are based on
   old and discredited accounts from just a couple of papers.  Oxnard
   himself considered _Australopithecus_ to be a human ancestor (Groves
   1999).  The vastly greater evidence that those species are fully
   bipedal and had other humanlike traits is either ignored, distorted,
   or baselessly dismissed by creationists (Foley 1997).
