BriefClaim: If we are descended from apes, why are there still apes around?
Title: Why are there still apes around?
Keywords: anagenesis, evolution, common descent.

Claim:

If we are descended from apes, why are there still apes around?

Source:

Robinson, B. A, 2003.  17 indicators that evolution didn't happen (with
 rebuttals). https://web.archive.org/web/20220408003735if_/http://www.religioustolerance.org/ev_noway.htm#11

Response:

1. Humans and other apes are descended from a common ancestor whose
   population split to become two (and more) lineages.  The question is
   rather like asking, "If many Americans and Australians are descended
   from Europeans, why are there still Europeans around?"
   Creationists themselves recognize the invalidity of this claim (AIG
   n.d.).
