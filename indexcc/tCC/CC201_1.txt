BriefClaim: Punctuated equilibrium was ad hoc to justify gaps.
Title: Punctuated equilibrium for the gaps
Keywords: phyletic gradualism, punctuated equilibrium, fossil, gap

Claim:

The theory of punctuated equilibrium was proposed ad hoc to explain away
the embarrassing gaps in the fossil record.

Source:

Yahya, Harun, 2003.  _Darwinism Refuted_, The invalidity of punctuated
 equilibrium. 
 https://web.archive.org/web/20200629032912if_/http://www.darwinismrefuted.com/equilibrium.html

Response:

1. The theory of punctuated equilibrium is based on positive evidence,
   including extensive studies of living and extinct species groups
   (Eldredge and Gould 1972).

2. The idea of phyletic gradualism, which is invoked to justify a lack of
   gaps, <CC201>fails to fit the evidence> of population biology.

3. There is nothing wrong with proposing theories to fit the data.
