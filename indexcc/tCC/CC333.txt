Title: Swansea Head fossil trees.
Keywords: polystrate, catastrophism

Claim:

Fossil trees at Swansea Head, N.S.W., Australia, jut out of a coal seam,
through ashy sediments, and through an upper coal layer.  The trees have
no roots.  This is evidence of catastrophic burial, since it is similar to
logs deposited in Spirit Lake after the Mt. St. Helens eruption.
