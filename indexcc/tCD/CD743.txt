Title: Movement of tectonic plates
Keywords: plate tectonics, GPS, VLBI

Claim:

A problem with the theory of plate tectonics is that there is no solid
evidence that tectonic plates are still moving.

Source:

CreationWiki. 2005 (27 June).  Continental drift.
 https://web.archive.org/web/20060301153445if_/http://www.nwcreation.net/wiki/index.php?title=Continental_drift

Response:

1. Few creationist claims show such a blatant denial of reality.  GPS and
   VBLI (Very Long Baseline Interferometry) allow measurements of
   positions more than precise enough to verify movement over time.  Even
   more obvious, earthquakes often move the plates several feet at once.
   The 26 December 2004 Sumatra earthquake produced a slip averaging more
   than five meters along the length of the rupture (Banerjee et
   al. 2005).  One can see the shift as twisted train rails or broken and
   offset fences in pictures of major earthquakes of the past.  The
   Calaveras fault in Hollister, California creeps continuously, varying
   by time and location from about 6 to 15 mm per year (Rogers and Nason).
