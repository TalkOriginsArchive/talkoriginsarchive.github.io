BriefClaim: NASA scientists found a day missing.
Title: Missing day
Keywords: urban legend, NASA

Claim:

NASA scientists, using computers to track planetary motions, discovered
that a day of time was missing, corresponding to biblical accounts of the
sun's standing still for Joshua for almost a day, plus the sun moving
backwards forty minutes for Hezekiah.

Source:

Hawaii Christians Online, n.d.  Missing day.
 https://web.archive.org/web/20210422171718if_/http://www.hawaiichristiansonline.com/the_missing_day.html

Response:

1. The origin of this urban legend goes back to 1890.  It is entirely
   baseless.  Indeed, it could not be true.  There is no frame of
   reference to measure against to determine whether a day was missing
   thousands of years ago.

Links:

Mikkelson, B. and David P. Mikkelson, 2000.  The lost day.
 http://www.snopes.com/religion/lostday.htm
