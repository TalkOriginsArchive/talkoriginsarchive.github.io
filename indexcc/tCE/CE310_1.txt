BriefClaim: Helmholtz's contraction theory says the sun is young.
Title: Helmholtz's solar contraction theory
Keywords: sun

Claim:

Helmholtz proposed that the sun's energy comes from gravitational
contraction.  Under this theory, the sun could not be more than about 20
million years old.

Source:

McDowell, Josh and Don Stewart, 1981.  _Reasons Skeptics should consider
 Christianity_.  San Bernardino, CA: Here's Life Publishers, Inc.
 https://web.archive.org/web/20250817130902if_/http://www.greatcom.org/resources/reasons_skeptics/ch_12/default.htm

Response:

1. Helmholtz's theory has been discredited.  It is now known that the sun
   gets its energy from nuclear fusion.

   Even ignoring the evidence for fusion, gravitational contraction fails
   for other reasons.  It does not explain the relationship between
   stellar temperatures and luminosity, and it is inconsistent with
   variable stars.
