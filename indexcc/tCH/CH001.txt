BriefClaim: Creationism has explanatory power.
Title: Explanatory
Keywords: theory, testability, design, accommodation.

Claim:

Creationism is explanatory.  It can accommodate all the results of
evolution and more.  In particular, it can also explain the results of a
designer.

Source:

Dembski, William A., 2001.  Is intelligent design testable?
 http://www.arn.org/docs/dembski/wd_isidtestable.htm

Response:

1. Accommodation is very different from explanation.  An explanation tells
   why something is one way and not another.  A theory that accommodates
   anything explains nothing, because it does not rule out any
   possibilities.  Accommodating all possibilities also makes a theory
   exactly useless.  Since creationism accommodates all possibilities, it
   is not explanatory.
