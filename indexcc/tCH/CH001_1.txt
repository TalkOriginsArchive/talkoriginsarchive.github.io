Title: Maury and Biblical ocean currents
Keywords: Bible, explanatory power, ocean currents, Matthew Maury

Claim:

realized nobody knew about paths of the sea.  On the basis of the Bible's
statement that they are there, he set out to find them.  He charted many
ocean currents and became the father of oceanographic navigation.
