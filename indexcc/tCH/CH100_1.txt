BriefClaim: Science must be interpreted in light of scripture.
Title: Science in light of Scripture
Keywords: education, teacher, evolution

Claim:

All scientific observations must be interpreted in light of Scripture.

Source:

Morris, John D. 1996.  ICR, for such a time as this.  _Back to Genesis_
 87a (Mar.).
 http://www.icr.org/index.php?module=articles&action=view&ID=798

Response:

1. Evolution is already compatible with Scripture, according to many
   religions (NCSE n.d.).

2. Interpreting science according to Scripture would require that all
   scientists and science instructors have extensive theology training.
   It would further require that they all agree on the proper biblical
   exegesis.  Creationists may find these requirements not only excessive,
   but also dangerous, since the interpretation of Scripture which the
   scientists come to accept could easily disagree with their own.
