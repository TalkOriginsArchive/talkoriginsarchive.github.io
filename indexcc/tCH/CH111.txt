Title: Ezekiel's prophecy of Tyre
Keywords: Bible, prophecy

Claim:

Ezekiel's prophecy of the destruction of Tyre (Ezekiel 26) contains
details which are best explained by divine inspiration:
 - Nebuchadnezzar will destroy the mainland of Tyre (Ezekiel 26:8).
 - Many nations come against Tyre in waves (26:3).
 - Tyre will be made a bare rock (26:4).
 - Fishermen will spread their nets on the site (26:5).
 - The remains of Tyre will be thrown into the sea (26:12).
 - Tyre will never be rebuilt. (26:14).
