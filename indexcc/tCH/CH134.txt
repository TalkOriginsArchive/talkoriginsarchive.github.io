BriefClaim: Records say civilization was man's original state.
Title: Civilization originally
Keywords: civilization

Claim:

Records say civilization was man's original condition.  Written records
converge on an origin about 3000 B.C.E.  Other dating methods are
uncertain.

Source:

Bible Study Manuals, n.d.  Creationism vs evolution (cont.)
 http://www.biblestudymanuals.net/k43.htm#IIB6e
Titcomb, J. H., 1871.  On the antiquity of civilization.  _Journal of the
 Transactions of The Victoria Institute_, Journal of 1870-71, pp. 3-25.
 http://www.creationism.org/victoria/VictoriaInst1870_pg001.htm

Response:

1. Written records began after civilization; they do not record man's
   original state.  Human fossils, however, show that humans are older
   than writing or other indications of civilization.  Many of the dating
   methods showing man's prehistoric antiquity are verified, among other
   ways, by comparison with written history (e.g., Renne et al. 1997).
