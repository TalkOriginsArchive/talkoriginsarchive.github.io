BriefClaim: There was no death or decay before the Fall.
Title: No death before the Fall
Keywords: death, decay, Fall, Adam and Eve.

Claim:

There was no death or decay before the Fall of Adam and Eve, when sin came
into the world.

Source:

Morris, John D., 2000.  Creation's Easter message.  _Acts & Facts_
 29(4) (Apr.): 1-2.
SciCre, p. 211.

Response:

1. The Biblical references to death refer to spiritual death.  Romans
   6:23, for example, says God, through Christ, already gave us eternal
   life.  Obviously, physical death still occurs, so Christ did not save
   mankind from it.  If the death from which Christ was supposed to save
   us was physical death, then Christ was a failure.

2. A world without death would be far from Edenic.  Either there would be
   no possibility of new birth, in which case none of us could be alive
   today, or the world would become so overpopulated with living things
   that most people would be buried alive under others.  Death is a
   necessary part of life.
