BriefClaim: Hardwicke Knight found soft wood timbers on Ararat.
Title: Hardwicke Knight's wood from Ararat
Keywords: arkeology

Claim:

In 1936, Hardwicke Knight was kidnapped on Ararat but later released.
While lost on Ararat, he found timbers of dark, soft wood.  This was on
the north side of the mountain, just below the ice pack.

Source:

LaHaye, Tim and John Morris, 1976. _The Ark on Ararat_, Nashville: Thomas
 Nelson Inc. and Creation Life Publishers, pp. 98-101.

Response:

1. According to the account, the wood was so soft that it quickly
   deteriorated.  This contrasts with all other accounts of the Ark, and
   it leaves no evidence to support the story.

2. Knight himself did not associate the wood with the Ark until years
   later.  Given that <CH504.1>other wood> had been carried up Ararat,
   Knight's wood could have been something else.
