BriefClaim: Juveniles of large animals were taken aboard.
Title: Juvenile animals on the Ark
Keywords: Noah's Ark

Claim:

There was room for all the animals aboard Noah's ark because juveniles of
the largest animals (animals larger than about 10 kg = 22 lb.) were taken.

Source:

Woodmorappe, John, 1996.  _Noah's Ark: A Feasibility Study_, Santee,
 CA: ICR, p. 13.

Response:

1. Even assuming the largest animals were juveniles, the ark would still
   have been <CH512>overcrowded>.

2. Noah was instructed to take "the male and its mate," implying sexual
   maturity of the animals (Gen. 7:2).  Juveniles do not have mates.

3. Juveniles must still be old enough to be weaned and, in some cases,
   socialized to learn behaviors from their parents.  This would make many
   of the animals old enough to be mostly grown already.
