Title: Sphinx eroded by flood.
Keywords: Sphinx, flood

Claim:

The Egyptian Sphinx, and the area immediately around it, show evidence of
water erosion.  Some suggest that this was caused by rising groundwater,
but there is evidence that the erosion occurred early and has not occurred
any further.

Source:

The Flood
