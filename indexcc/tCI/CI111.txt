BriefClaim: Dembski's filter can detect design.
Title: Explanatory Filter
Keywords: design, information, complexity, specification, explanatory
  filter.

Claim:

Design can be recognized by the following filter:

  1. If an event E has high probability, accept regularity as an
     explanation; otherwise move to the next step.
  2. If the chance hypothesis assigns E a high probability or E is
     not specified, then accept chance; otherwise move down the list.
  3. Having eliminated regularity and chance, accept design.
