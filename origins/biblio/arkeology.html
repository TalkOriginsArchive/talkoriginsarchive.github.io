TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Arkeology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/arkeology.html

Arkeology refers to the search for evidence of Noah's Ark, a central claim in young-earth creationism. This bibliography gathers sources from creationist expeditions, theological interpretations, and historical accounts, including works by John Morris and Henry Morris. It also includes scholarly analyses of flood myths and biblical narratives, highlighting the intersection of religious belief, pseudoscience, and deep-time geology in the creation/evolution debate.
