Astronomy

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/astronomy.html

Astronomy encompasses the study of celestial bodies and phenomena, including stars, planets, and galaxies. This bibliography gathers sources that explore the origins and evolution of the universe, solar system, and Earth, addressing topics like stellar formation, cosmic evolution, and the history of astronomical observations. It also includes works relevant to the creation/evolution controversy, such as critiques of pseudoscientific claims about celestial events and their historical interpretations.

Abstract

We have used the ASCA and ROSAT X-ray satellites to probe the coronae of a sample of nine solar-like G stars.
