TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Astrophysics

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/astrophysics.html

Astrophysics encompasses the study of celestial objects, space, and the physical universe as a whole. This bibliography includes sources on cosmic phenomena, stellar evolution, and the origins of the universe, providing insights into deep time and the formation of galaxies. It also touches on the distribution of asteroids, relevant to understanding the early solar system, and addresses concepts like self-organized criticality, which have implications for complex systems in both astrophysics and earth history.
