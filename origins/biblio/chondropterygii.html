TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Chondropterygii

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/chondropterygii.html

Chondropterygii refers to a subclass of cartilaginous fish, including sharks and rays, which have been extensively cataloged in natural history collections. This bibliography gathers sources from the British Museum's early specimen lists and later taxonomic works, reflecting the historical development of ichthyology. These citations provide insights into the classification and evolution of cartilaginous fish, relevant to understanding deep time and the fossil record in the context of the origins of life.
