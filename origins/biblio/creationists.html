TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Creationists

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/creationists.html

Creationists represent individuals and groups who advocate for the literal interpretation of religious texts, particularly the Bible, as the primary explanation for the origins of life, Earth, and the universe. Their work often challenges the scientific consensus on evolution, deep time, and the geological history of the Earth. This bibliography includes sources that examine creationist claims, their interaction with the scientific community, and the legal and educational controversies surrounding the creation/evolution debate.
