TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Dendrochronology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/dendrochronology.html

Dendrochronology is the scientific method of dating trees and past environmental conditions through the analysis of tree ring patterns. This bibliography gathers sources that explore the technique’s applications in understanding deep time, climate history, and the evolution of plant species, particularly the maidenhair tree (Ginkgo biloba). It also addresses claims about the tree’s longevity and historical significance within the broader context of origins and earth history debates.
