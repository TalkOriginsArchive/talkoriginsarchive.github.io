TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Egypt

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/egypt.html

Egypt is a key site for paleontological discoveries, particularly in the field of dinosaur paleontology. Ernst Stromer's extensive research expeditions in the Western Desert of Egypt yielded significant finds, including the original Spinosaurus and Carcharodontosaurus fossils. These sources contribute to understanding deep time and the evolution of dinosaurs, offering valuable evidence in the broader context of earth history and the origins of life.
