TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Endocrinology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/endocrinology.html

Endocrinology is the study of hormones and their functions in organisms, including regulation of metabolism, growth, and behavior. This bibliography gathers sources that explore endocrinological aspects across various taxa, from protochordates to vertebrates, highlighting evolutionary adaptations and physiological mechanisms. It includes studies on hormone regulation in osmoregulation, behavior, and population dynamics, contributing to our understanding of biological processes over deep time and their relevance to the creation/evolution controversy.
