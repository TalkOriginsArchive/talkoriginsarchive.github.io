TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Eurasia

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/eurasia.html

Eurasia refers to the combined landmass of Europe and Asia, a region central to studies of paleontology, archaeology, and evolutionary biology. This bibliography gathers sources that explore the evolutionary history of species, particularly elephants, across Eurasia during the Quaternary period. It also includes works on linguistic and cultural evolution, reflecting the diverse scholarly interest in the deep historical and biological context of this vast region.
