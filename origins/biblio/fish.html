TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Fish

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/fish.html

Fish refers to a diverse group of aquatic vertebrates that have played a crucial role in the study of evolution and paleontology. This bibliography includes sources on fish anatomy, physiology, and behavior, as well as their fossil record, which provides key evidence for understanding deep time and the origins of vertebrates. Works by Hoar, Randall, and others explore the environmental adaptations and evolutionary relationships of fish, contributing to the broader discussion on the transition from aquatic to terrestrial life.
