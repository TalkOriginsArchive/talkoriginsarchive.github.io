TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Gastropods

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/gastropods.html

Gastropods are a diverse class of mollusks that include snails and slugs. This bibliography compiles sources focusing on the evolutionary history and stratigraphic distribution of gastropods, particularly from the Eocene and Upper Cretaceous periods. These studies contribute to understanding deep time and the fossil record, relevant to both earth history and the origins debate. Citations include detailed analyses of gastropod species from Texas and Wyoming, offering insights into paleoenvironments and evolutionary patterns.
