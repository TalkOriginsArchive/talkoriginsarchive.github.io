TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Genesis

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/genesis.html

Genesis refers to the first book of the Bible, which contains creation stories central to discussions about origins, deep time, and the creation/evolution controversy. This bibliography includes sources that explore interpretations of Genesis by religious and scientific figures, such as Thomas Huxley’s critiques and Gladstone’s views. These citations highlight historical debates over how Genesis relates to scientific understandings of nature and evolution.
