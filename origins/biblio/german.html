TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

German

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/german.html

German sources in this bibliography cover a range of paleontological and evolutionary studies, particularly focusing on the fossil record from Germany. These include detailed analyses of Mesozoic ecosystems, dinosaur origins, and biostratigraphy. The literature also explores broader evolutionary trends and historical geology, contributing to our understanding of deep time and the development of life. These citations are relevant to the origins debate, offering scientific insights into the fossil evidence for evolution and earth history.
