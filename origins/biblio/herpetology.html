TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Herpetology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/herpetology.html

Herpetology is the branch of zoology concerned with amphibians and reptiles. This bibliography collects sources on their morphology, ecology, and evolutionary history, including fossil records and comparative anatomy. These works provide evidence for deep time and biological change, relevant to understanding vertebrate evolution and addressing creationist claims about species origins.
