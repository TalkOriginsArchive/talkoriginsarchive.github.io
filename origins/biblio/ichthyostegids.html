TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Ichthyostegids

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/ichthyostegids.html

Ichthyostegids were early tetrapods that provide critical evidence for the evolutionary transition from water to land during the Devonian period. This bibliography collects sources on these fossils, which are central to understanding vertebrate origins and deep time. The citations include paleontological studies and historical references that address scientific claims about early tetrapod anatomy and phylogeny.
