About this bibliography

The original Archive bibliography distributed Martin R. Leipzig's 1993 keyword-indexed bibliography for the creation/evolution controversy. Leipzig described it as a third-generation, continually updated register of keyword-indexed entries using Geological Society of America citation style, and requested acknowledgement if the list was redistributed. The Archive continues to retain that attribution.

The current system starts from those legacy topic pages and then rebuilds them as a normalized topic corpus. The restoration process parsed the GSA-style citations, expanded repeated-author dash notation, deduplicated repeated records, and preserved the original keyword organization so older topic routes still work.

CiteGeist then attempted to improve the records by checking citation metadata through Crossref, OpenAlex, PubMed, and DBLP; adding DOI links and source links where available; adding abstracts where they could be located and displayed appropriately; and exporting BibTeX-formatted bibliography files for most topics. OpenAlex citation-graph expansion also added newer connected works where the metadata supported the topic.

The result is a reviewable bibliography aid rather than a claim that every enriched record has had final editorial review. Original Leipzig coverage is retained, newer metadata and source links are added where available, and records that need correction or additional provenance can be handled through the Workbench process.
