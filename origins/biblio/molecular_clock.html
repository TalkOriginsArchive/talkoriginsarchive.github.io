TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Molecular clock

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/molecular_clock.html

Molecular clock refers to the technique of using mutation rates in biomolecules to deduce the time in prehistory when two or more life forms diverged. This concept is central to estimating divergence times in evolutionary biology and deep time, often intersecting with debates on the reliability of dating methods within the creation/evolution controversy. The bibliography includes sources discussing the method’s principles, historical conflicts over results, and its application to human evolution.
