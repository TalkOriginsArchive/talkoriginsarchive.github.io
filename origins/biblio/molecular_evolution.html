TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Molecular evolution

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/molecular_evolution.html

Molecular evolution examines genetic changes over deep time, providing critical evidence for common ancestry and informing estimates of divergence dates through molecular clocks. This bibliography includes foundational texts on phylogenetics and the origin of life, alongside resources addressing the scientific method and historical debates within the creation/evolution controversy.
