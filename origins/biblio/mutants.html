TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Mutants

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/mutants.html

Mutants refers to organisms with altered genetic material, a concept central to evolutionary biology and the study of variation. This bibliography includes sources on genetic mutations, such as SARS-CoV-2 variants, and historical references to morphological changes in fossils. It also contains citations where "mutants" appears in unrelated contexts, such as existentialism or clinical forms, reflecting the term's broad usage in literature.
