TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Natural resources

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/natural_resources.html

Natural resources covers the management, conservation, and societal impact of Earth's materials. This bibliography includes sources on landscape deterioration, such as Blake’s critique of planned obsolescence, alongside scholarly works on resource policy and sustainability. It addresses how human interaction with these resources intersects with environmental ethics and historical debates on stewardship.
