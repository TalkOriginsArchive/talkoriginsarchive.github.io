TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nautiloids

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nautiloids.html

Nautiloids are an extinct group of cephalopods that serve as important index fossils for correlating sedimentary rock layers and understanding deep time. This bibliography includes paleontological classifications and studies on their growth patterns, such as those relating to the Earth-Moon system, which provide evidence for long-term biological and geological processes. These sources support the scientific understanding of evolutionary history and stratigraphy against creationist claims of rapid formation.
