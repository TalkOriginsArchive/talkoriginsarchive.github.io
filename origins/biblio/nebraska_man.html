TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nebraska man

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nebraska_man.html

Nebraska man refers to the fossil fragment Hesperopithecus, once erroneously identified as a human ancestor but later recognized as pig jawbone. This bibliography documents the case’s prominent role in the creation-evolution controversy, particularly how creationists have cited it to challenge scientific integrity and evolutionary theory. The sources here examine the historical context, the scientific correction, and the ongoing rhetorical use of this episode in debates over origins and deep time.
