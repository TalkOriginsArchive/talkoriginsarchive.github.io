TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nemesis theory

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nemesis_theory.html

Nemesis theory proposes a hypothetical companion star to the Sun that periodically perturbs comets, potentially causing mass extinctions on Earth. This bibliography collects sources discussing the scientific hypothesis, its historical development, and critiques within the context of deep time and evolutionary history. It includes reviews of key literature, such as David Raup’s work, addressing the theory’s role in explaining geological patterns and its reception in the broader scientific community.
