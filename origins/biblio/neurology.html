TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Neurology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/neurology.html

Neurology covers the study of the nervous system, with this bibliography focusing on its evolutionary history and comparative anatomy in vertebrates. The selected sources examine how brain structure and behavior have evolved across different species, providing evidence for deep time and common ancestry. This literature supports scientific understanding of biological origins while countering creationist claims that deny evolutionary relationships in neural development.
