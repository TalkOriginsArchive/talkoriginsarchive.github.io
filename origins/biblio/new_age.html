TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

New age

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/new_age.html

New age refers to a spiritual movement often associated with parapsychology and the occult, topics frequently critiqued in the context of the creation/evolution controversy and scientific skepticism. This bibliography includes sources analyzing the movement's claims and its historical intersection with fringe science, such as parapsychology research. It serves to provide critical perspectives on these non-scientific beliefs within the broader discussion of origins and the scientific method.
