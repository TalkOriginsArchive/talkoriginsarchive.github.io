TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

New england

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/new_england.html

New england serves as a geographic reference point for historical paleontological studies, most notably Edward Hitchcock’s 1848 work on fossil footprints in the region. While the bibliography includes this foundational text on trace fossils, the remaining citations are largely unrelated, covering diverse biological subjects from marine invertebrates to modern integrative systems biology. This collection reflects the site’s broader interest in the history of science and the interpretation of fossil evidence within the context of origins debates.
