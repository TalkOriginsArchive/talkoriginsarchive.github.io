TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

New guinea

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/new_guinea.html

New guinea refers to the Huon Peninsula region, cited here for its well-documented coral terraces that provide critical evidence for Quaternary tectonic movements and Holocene sea-level changes. These geological studies support scientific understandings of deep time and earth history, offering empirical data relevant to the broader context of origins science and the evaluation of creationist claims regarding recent geological events.
