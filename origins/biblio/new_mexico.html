TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

New mexico

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/new_mexico.html

New mexico serves as a key geographic region for understanding deep time and vertebrate evolution, particularly through the rich fossil record of the San Juan Basin. This bibliography compiles geological and paleontological sources that document Late Cretaceous and early Tertiary strata, providing essential context for the origins of dinosaur faunas and sedimentary environments. These citations support the scientific study of earth history and stratigraphy in the American Southwest.
