TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nobel laureates

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nobel_laureates.html

Nobel laureates are frequently cited in creationist arguments to assert that prominent scientists reject evolution. This bibliography collects sources addressing these claims, including lists of laureates and their stated views. It serves to contextualize how scientific authority is invoked in the creation/evolution controversy and to evaluate the accuracy of such appeals to prestige.
