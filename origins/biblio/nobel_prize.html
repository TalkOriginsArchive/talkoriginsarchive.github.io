TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nobel prize

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nobel_prize.html

Nobel prize references the prestigious international awards often cited in debates over scientific authority and the creation/evolution controversy. This bibliography includes the 1986 Amicus Curiae Brief by Nobel Laureates supporting evolution education, alongside historical records and biographical sources of laureates. It serves as a resource for examining how scientific recognition is invoked in public discourse regarding origins and deep time.
