TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Nova scotia

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/nova_scotia.html

Nova scotia is a Canadian province renowned for its rich fossil record, which provides critical evidence for deep time and evolutionary history. The bibliography cites geological and paleontological studies, such as those on Permian-Triassic transitions and recent paleoproteomics, that illuminate ancient biological changes. These sources support scientific understanding of earth history against creationist claims of a young earth.
