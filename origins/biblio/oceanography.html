TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Oceanography

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/oceanography.html

Oceanography is the scientific study of the physical, chemical, and biological properties of the oceans. This bibliography collects sources on marine science, including historical reviews and physical chemistry, which provide context for understanding Earth's deep time and environmental history. While primarily a natural science field, these works are relevant to discussions of origins and earth history, particularly regarding past ocean states and geological processes.
