TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Olduvai

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/olduvai.html

Olduvai refers to the Olduvai Gorge in Tanzania, a pivotal site for paleoanthropology and the study of human evolution. This bibliography collects sources documenting the discovery of early hominid fossils and the geological dating of the region, which are central to understanding deep time and the origins of Homo. The citations primarily reflect the scientific evidence used to counter creationist claims regarding the age of humanity and the Earth.
