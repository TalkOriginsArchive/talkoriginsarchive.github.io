TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Osmoregulation

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/osmoregulation.html

Osmoregulation refers to the physiological processes by which organisms maintain water and salt balance. This bibliography compiles scientific literature on vertebrate osmoregulation, primarily focusing on comparative physiology and endocrine control. While these studies provide detailed mechanistic insights into biological adaptation, they do not directly address origins, deep time, or the creation/evolution controversy. The collection serves as a reference for the biological mechanisms of homeostasis rather than evolutionary history.
