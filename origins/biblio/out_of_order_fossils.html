TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Out of order fossils

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/out_of_order_fossils.html

Out of order fossils refers to the creationist claim that fossils appear in the geological record in a sequence inconsistent with evolutionary theory, often citing specific anomalies like alleged human remains in ancient strata. This bibliography addresses such arguments, which are central to the creation/evolution controversy and public debates over deep time. It includes sources analyzing these claims, such as the Fort Union controversy, to evaluate their validity within the scientific method and earth history.
