TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Oysters

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/oysters.html

Oysters are bivalve mollusks frequently cited in paleontological literature to illustrate evolutionary patterns, such as speciation and morphological change over deep time. This bibliography focuses on scientific studies of oyster fossils and taxonomy, which provide evidence for earth history and biological evolution. It excludes unrelated species sharing similar names, such as certain frogs or insects, to maintain relevance to origins and evolutionary science.
