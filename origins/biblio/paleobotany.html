TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Paleobotany

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/paleobotany.html

Paleobotany is the study of fossil plants, providing critical evidence for the history of life, deep time, and the evolution of terrestrial ecosystems. This bibliography collects sources on plant fossils, ancient floras, and their significance for understanding earth history and biological change. It serves as a reference for examining how paleobotanical data informs scientific models of origins and counters creationist claims regarding the age and development of life.
