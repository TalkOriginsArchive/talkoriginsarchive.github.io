TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Paleozoic

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/paleozoic.html

Paleozoic refers to the geologic era spanning approximately 541 to 252 million years ago, a critical period for understanding deep time, the diversification of early life, and the formation of major geological features. This bibliography curates scientific literature on stratigraphy, paleontology, and earth history relevant to evolutionary biology and the origins of life. It serves as a resource for examining evidence often cited in discussions regarding the age of the Earth and the fossil record.

Abstract

Bauxites which often occur on the Paleozoic basement surface are of rather a complex mineralogical composition. Diaspore, boehmite, siderite, kaolinite, crandallite and other minerals are identified in them. Their structural and textural characteristic are described. The laterite-sedimentary genesis of the rocks is concluded. The siderite found in Urman bauxites is assigned to the oil deposit presence.
