TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Pauling

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pauling.html

Pauling refers to Linus Pauling, a Nobel Prize-winning chemist whose work on molecular biology and the chemical nature of genes contributed to the understanding of life's origins. This bibliography collects sources discussing his scientific legacy, his advocacy for peace, and his later controversial views on health and evolution. It serves as a reference for understanding his complex role in the history of science and the creation/evolution controversy.
