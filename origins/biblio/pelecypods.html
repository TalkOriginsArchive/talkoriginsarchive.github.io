Pelecypods

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pelecypods.html

Pelecypods, commonly known as bivalve mollusks, are a significant group of marine invertebrates used extensively in stratigraphy and evolutionary studies. This bibliography compiles sources on their fossil succession, speciation, and Paleozoic diversity, supporting research into deep time and biological evolution. The included citations primarily address geological history and taxonomic classification rather than creationist claims.
