Petroleum geology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/petroleum_geology.html

Petroleum geology is the study of the origin, migration, and accumulation of hydrocarbons within sedimentary basins. This bibliography includes sources on basin formation, stratigraphy, and the geological history of oil and gas fields, which are relevant to understanding deep time and earth history. While primarily a scientific discipline, these citations may be referenced in discussions regarding the age of the Earth or the scientific method in the context of the creation/evolution controversy.
