TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Photography

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/photography.html

Photography is included here primarily due to its historical role in the creation/evolution controversy, specifically regarding the 1985 analysis of the Archaeopteryx fossil in the British Journal of Photography. While the bibliography also contains general sources on photographic technique and realism, these are less central to the site’s scientific focus. This collection highlights how visual evidence and its interpretation have been debated in origins discussions.
