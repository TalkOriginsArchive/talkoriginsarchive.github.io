Physics

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/physics.html

Physics provides the fundamental framework for understanding deep time, thermodynamics, and the physical laws governing the universe's origin and evolution. This bibliography includes sources on radiometric dating, entropy, and the historical development of physical theories, alongside discussions of creationist claims regarding physics. It serves as a resource for examining how physical principles inform scientific explanations of origins and earth history.
