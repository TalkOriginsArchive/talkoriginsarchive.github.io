TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Physiology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/physiology.html

Physiology refers to the study of normal functions in living systems. This bibliography compiles foundational texts and reference works on animal and human biological processes. These sources provide essential background for understanding evolutionary adaptations and comparative anatomy. They are frequently cited in discussions regarding the complexity of life and the mechanisms of natural selection.
