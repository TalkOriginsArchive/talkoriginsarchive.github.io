TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Piltdown man

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/piltdown_man.html

Piltdown man refers to the infamous early-twentieth-century paleoanthropological hoax that temporarily misled researchers about human origins. This bibliography collects sources examining the fraud, its detection, and its impact on the scientific method and public understanding of evolution. It highlights how the controversy influenced debates on deep time and human evolution history.
