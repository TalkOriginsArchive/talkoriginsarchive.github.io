Pineal gland

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pineal_gland.html

Pineal gland collects sources on the anatomy and evolution of the pineal complex, primarily focusing on comparative vertebrate biology and lamprey studies. While the pineal organ is relevant to understanding deep time and evolutionary history, these citations largely address its physiological role in non-human species rather than human origins or creationist controversies. The collection serves as a reference for the gland's biological function across diverse taxa.
