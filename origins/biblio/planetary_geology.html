Planetary geology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/planetary_geology.html

Planetary geology is the scientific study of the geology of planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. This bibliography collects sources on the physical processes and geological evolution of celestial bodies within our solar system. While primarily a sub-discipline of planetary science, it provides context for understanding Earth's formation and the broader principles of planetary evolution. The citations here focus on introductory texts and reviews rather than direct arguments in the creation/evolution controversy.
