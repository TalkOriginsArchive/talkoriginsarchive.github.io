TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Pliocene

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pliocene.html

Pliocene is the geologic epoch spanning approximately 5.3 to 2.6 million years ago, serving as a critical interval for understanding recent climate shifts, glacial cycles, and the evolution of hominins. This bibliography collects scientific literature on Pliocene stratigraphy, paleoclimatology, and tectonic history, often cited in discussions of deep time and Earth's environmental past. It provides evidence relevant to the scientific method and historical geology, distinct from creationist claims.
