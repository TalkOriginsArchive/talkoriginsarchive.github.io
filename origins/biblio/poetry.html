TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Poetry

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/poetry.html

Poetry serves as a cultural lens through which historical figures have processed concepts of deep time, human origins, and the natural world. This bibliography curates literary works, such as Tennyson’s In Memoriam and Whitman’s Leaves of Grass, that intersect with the creation/evolution controversy and public understanding of science. These sources illustrate how artistic expression has historically engaged with scientific ideas, offering context for the humanities side of the origins debate.
