TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Polypeptides

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/polypeptides.html

Polypeptides refers to chains of amino acids, central to biochemistry and the study of life's origins. This bibliography includes historical research on thermal polypeptides, which explored abiotic synthesis of protein-like molecules in prebiotic contexts. While some sources address fundamental chemical processes relevant to earth history, others focus on industrial or general synthetic applications. The collection serves as a reference for understanding the scientific literature on these molecules rather than supporting specific creationist claims.
