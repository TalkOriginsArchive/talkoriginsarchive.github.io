TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Poposauridae

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/poposauridae.html

Poposauridae is a family of pseudosuchian archosaurs, often historically misidentified as dinosaurs or "thecodonts," that lived during the Triassic period. This bibliography addresses their classification within the broader context of vertebrate evolution and deep time, distinguishing them from true dinosaurs. It serves to clarify taxonomic confusion in the history of paleontology and correct misconceptions often exploited in creationist arguments regarding the fossil record.
