TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Possession

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/possession.html

Possession refers to the historical and cultural concept of spiritual or demonic control, often cited in creationist arguments to explain unexplained phenomena or as a counterpoint to naturalistic explanations of mental illness. This bibliography examines the intersection of these claims with scientific understanding, particularly through the lens of neuropathology and psychology. It includes sources that analyze the legacy of such beliefs in anthropology, history, and theology, while also addressing how modern science interprets these experiences.
