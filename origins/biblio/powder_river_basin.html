TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Powder river basin

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/powder_river_basin.html

Powder river basin refers to a major geological and coal-producing region in Wyoming and Montana. This bibliography collects sources on its stratigraphy, fossil zones, and resource extraction, primarily from geological literature. While some citations are unrelated noise, the core references address earth history and deep time, relevant to understanding regional geology rather than creationist claims.
