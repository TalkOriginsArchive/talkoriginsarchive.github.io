TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Pragmatism

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pragmatism.html

Pragmatism is a philosophical tradition emphasizing practical consequences and instrumental truth, often contrasted with rigid materialism. In the context of origins and earth history, this bibliography highlights its role in stratigraphic methodology and broader scientific epistemology. It also addresses how pragmatic approaches intersect with debates on faith, materialism, and the scientific method within the creation/evolution controversy.
