TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Primitive life

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/primitive_life.html

Primitive life refers to the earliest biological entities and the processes by which non-living matter transitioned into living systems. This bibliography addresses hypotheses regarding abiogenesis and the nature of the first organisms, topics central to understanding the origins of life and deep time. It includes sources discussing the scientific method in reconstructing early earth history and critiques of creationist claims that deny such ancient biological origins.
