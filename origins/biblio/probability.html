TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Probability

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/probability.html

Probability addresses the statistical arguments often invoked in the creation/evolution controversy, particularly regarding the likelihood of abiogenesis or complex biological structures arising by chance. This bibliography compiles sources that critique these "probability arguments" and discuss the role of chance in evolutionary theory, deep time, and origins of life debates. It includes both scientific analyses of thermodynamics and probability, as well as historical and sociological studies of antievolutionary rhetoric.
