Protarthropods

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/protarthropods.html

Protarthropods refer to a group of early arthropod-like organisms, often discussed in the context of the Cambrian explosion and the evolutionary origins of arthropods. This bibliography collects paleontological literature on their morphology and phylogenetic relationships, which are central to understanding deep time and the fossil record. These sources are relevant to the scientific study of earth history and the evidence for evolution, distinct from creationist claims.
