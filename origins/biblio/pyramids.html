TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Pyramids

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/pyramids.html

Pyramids serves as a keyword for sources addressing geological interpretations of ancient structures and biblical events, such as the Exodus plagues. This bibliography includes citations that examine earth history and deep time through a scientific lens, often contrasting with creationist claims. It provides references for understanding how geological evidence informs debates on origins and the historical context of ancient monuments.
