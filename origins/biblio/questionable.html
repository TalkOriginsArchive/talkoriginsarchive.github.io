TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Questionable

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/questionable.html

Questionable collects sources addressing dubious scientific claims, flawed experiments, and controversial theories often cited in creationist or pseudoscientific arguments. It includes critiques of works like Velikovsky’s catastrophism and reviews of theological texts, serving as a resource for evaluating the validity of arguments within the creation/evolution controversy. The collection highlights literature that challenges the scientific method or historical accuracy in origins discussions.
