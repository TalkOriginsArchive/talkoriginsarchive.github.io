TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rays

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rays.html

Rays refers to cartilaginous fish within the subclass Elasmobranchii, encompassing skates and chimaeras alongside sharks. This bibliography curates scientific literature on their phylogeny, genetics, and evolutionary history, providing evidence for deep time and biological classification. It serves as a resource for understanding the natural history of these organisms within the broader context of evolutionary biology and earth history.
