TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rebuttal

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rebuttal.html

Rebuttal collects sources that address specific scientific or methodological critiques, primarily within medical and environmental research contexts. While the term "rebuttal" appears in the titles of these citations, they do not directly engage with the creation/evolution controversy or origins science. The collection serves as a reference for how scientific literature handles peer correspondence and counter-arguments in specialized fields, rather than providing evidence for evolutionary theory.
