Reptile/bird transitions

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/reptile_bird_transitions.html

Reptile/bird transitions covers the fossil evidence and scientific debate surrounding the evolutionary link between theropod dinosaurs and modern birds, with a historical focus on Archaeopteryx. It includes sources addressing deep time, anatomical scaling, and the scientific method, while also documenting creationist challenges to these transitional forms. The collection serves as a reference for understanding the origins of avian flight and the broader context of vertebrate evolution.
