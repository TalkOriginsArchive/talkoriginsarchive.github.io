TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Reptilian biology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/reptilian_biology.html

Reptilian biology encompasses the physiological, morphological, and ecological study of reptiles, serving as a foundational reference for understanding vertebrate evolution and deep time. This bibliography cites key volumes from the Biology of the Reptilia series, which provide essential data on reptilian metabolism and development. These sources support scientific discussions on evolutionary history and are often referenced in debates concerning the creation/evolution controversy.
