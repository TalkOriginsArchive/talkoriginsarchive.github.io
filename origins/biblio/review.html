Review

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/review.html

Review collects critical assessments and scholarly critiques relevant to the creation/evolution controversy and scientific method. It includes sources evaluating creationist claims, such as critiques of ICR publications, alongside broader academic reviews in earth history and humanities. The collection highlights how scientific literature engages with and refutes antievolutionary arguments, providing context for understanding the debate over origins and deep time.
