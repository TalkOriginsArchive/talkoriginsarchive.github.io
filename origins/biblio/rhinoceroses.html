TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rhinoceroses

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rhinoceroses.html

Rhinoceroses serve as a classic case study in paleontology for illustrating evolutionary rates, trends, and macroevolutionary patterns. This bibliography focuses on scientific literature, particularly mid-20th century works like those by Simpson and Mayr, that use rhinoceros fossils to support deep time and gradual change. It addresses the scientific understanding of mammalian evolution rather than creationist claims.
