TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rhodesia

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rhodesia.html

Rhodesia refers to the former British colony now known as Zimbabwe and Zambia. This bibliography primarily addresses paleontological research from the region, specifically the discovery of dinosaur fossils in the Forest Sandstone. It serves as a reference for sources discussing the geological history and vertebrate fauna of southern Africa during the Mesozoic era.
