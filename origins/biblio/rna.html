TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rna

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rna.html

Rna refers to ribonucleic acid, a molecule central to modern biology and the "RNA world" hypothesis regarding the origin of life. This bibliography includes key historical sources, such as Gilbert’s 1986 proposal, alongside contemporary research on RNA catalysis and biogenesis. It serves as a resource for understanding the scientific evidence for life's chemical origins and the role of RNA in evolution, distinct from creationist claims.
