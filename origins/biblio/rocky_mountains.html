TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Rocky mountains

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/rocky_mountains.html

Rocky mountains collects sources on the geological formation, stratigraphy, and fossil record of the Rocky Mountains, particularly in Colorado. These citations address deep time, earth history, and the scientific evidence for evolution, often contrasting with creationist claims about the region's age. The collection includes historical geological surveys and modern studies relevant to understanding the area's natural history.
