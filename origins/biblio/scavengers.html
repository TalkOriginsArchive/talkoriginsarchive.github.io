TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Scavengers

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/scavengers.html

Scavengers refers to organisms that consume dead animal matter, a key ecological role in nutrient cycling and ecosystem dynamics. This bibliography primarily cites literature on Serengeti ecology, including foundational works by Sinclair and Norton-Griffiths, rather than focusing exclusively on scavenger biology. It serves as a reference for understanding the broader context of African ecosystems and their management, linking scavenger adaptations to deep-time ecological processes and conservation science.
