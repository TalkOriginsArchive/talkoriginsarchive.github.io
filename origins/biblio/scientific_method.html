TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Scientific method

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/scientific_method.html

Scientific method encompasses the logical and empirical frameworks used to investigate natural phenomena, including origins, evolution, and earth history. This bibliography curates philosophical and methodological sources that clarify how scientific inquiry distinguishes itself from creationist claims. It includes foundational texts on logic, explanation, and the nature of scientific theory relevant to the creation/evolution controversy.
