TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Seismic methods

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/seismic_methods.html

Seismic methods refer to techniques using sound waves to image subsurface geological structures, providing critical evidence for plate tectonics, deep time, and the Earth's internal composition. This bibliography collects sources on seismic imaging and data processing, which underpin modern geophysics and help refute creationist claims of a young Earth. While the collection includes technical papers on survey effectiveness, it primarily supports the scientific understanding of Earth's history and structure.
