TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Sensory systems

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/sensory_systems.html

Sensory systems encompasses the biological structures and mechanisms that detect and process environmental stimuli, with a particular focus here on the vestibular apparatus and semicircular canals. This bibliography curates scientific literature examining the functional significance, morphology, and physiology of these systems. It serves as a resource for understanding the evolution and development of sensory organs within the broader context of biological origins and earth history.
