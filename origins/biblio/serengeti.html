TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Serengeti

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/serengeti.html

Serengeti refers to the famous East African ecosystem, primarily known for its large mammal populations and complex predator-prey dynamics. This bibliography collects scientific literature on the region’s ecology, including studies on predators, scavengers, and forest maintenance by birds. These sources provide empirical data on evolutionary adaptations and ecosystem interactions, rather than addressing creationist claims.
