TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Silicates

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/silicates.html

Silicates are a broad class of minerals, but this bibliography focuses specifically on chert and flint, which are microcrystalline forms of silica. These materials are significant in earth history and archaeology, serving as key indicators of sedimentary processes and ancient human tool-making. The selected sources provide geological and archaeological context for these substances, rather than addressing broader geochemical debates.
