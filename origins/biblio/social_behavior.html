TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Social behavior

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/social_behavior.html

Social behavior encompasses the study of interactions among organisms, ranging from primate and avian communities to dinosaur gregariousness. This bibliography includes sources on the evolution of social organization, sexual selection, and behavioral ecology. It also references historical works on social evolution, providing context for how these concepts intersect with evolutionary theory and the history of science.
