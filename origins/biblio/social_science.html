TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Social science

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/social_science.html

Social science encompasses the study of human society and social relationships, providing essential context for understanding the cultural and historical dimensions of the creation/evolution controversy. This bibliography includes sources on the definition and scope of social sciences, as well as interdisciplinary works like E.O. Wilson’s Genes, Mind, and Culture, which explore the intersection of biology and culture. These citations help clarify how social scientific perspectives inform public discourse on origins and scientific method.
