TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Southwestern us

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/southwestern_us.html

Southwestern us covers scientific literature on the geology, paleontology, and evolutionary biology of the American Southwest. It includes studies on desert rodent camouflage and dinosaur fossils, illustrating regional evidence for deep time and natural selection. The collection supports discussions on earth history and the scientific method within the context of the creation/evolution controversy.
