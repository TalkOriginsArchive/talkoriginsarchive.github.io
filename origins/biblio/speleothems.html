TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Speleothems

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/speleothems.html

Speleothems are mineral deposits formed in caves, such as stalactites and stalagmites, which serve as valuable archives for reconstructing past climates and geological history. This bibliography collects sources on their formation and chemical composition, highlighting their role in establishing deep time and supporting scientific understandings of earth history. These references are often cited in discussions regarding the age of the Earth and the validity of radiometric dating methods within the creation/evolution controversy.
