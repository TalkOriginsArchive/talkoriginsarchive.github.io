Stochasticy

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/stochasticy.html

Stochasticy refers to the role of random processes in evolutionary biology, ecology, and paleontology. This bibliography collects sources on stochastic models used to explain diversity, morphological evolution, and population dynamics. These works are central to understanding how chance events interact with natural selection in deep time and modern ecological systems.
