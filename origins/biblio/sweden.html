TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Sweden

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/sweden.html

Sweden serves as a key locality for the "Orsten" Lagerstätte, a site renowned for exceptional preservation of soft-bodied organisms from the Upper Cambrian period. This bibliography collects scientific literature documenting these fossils, which provide critical evidence for early arthropod evolution and the rapid diversification of life during the Cambrian explosion. The references primarily focus on detailed morphological and phylogenetic analyses of crustaceans and conodonts found in Swedish strata.
