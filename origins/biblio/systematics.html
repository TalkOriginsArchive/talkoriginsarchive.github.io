TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Systematics

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/systematics.html

Systematics is the scientific study of the diversity of life and the relationships among organisms, providing the foundational framework for reconstructing evolutionary history and deep time. This bibliography highlights how systematic data and phylogenetic methods are used to test hypotheses about origins and to counter creationist claims. It includes key literature on cladistics, comparative biology, and the application of systematics in resolving the evolution-creation controversy.
