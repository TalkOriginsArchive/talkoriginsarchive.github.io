TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Systems analysis

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/systems_analysis.html

Systems analysis refers to the application of mathematical and computational modeling to understand complex, interacting components within biological and ecological networks. This bibliography collects sources on systems ecology and the theoretical frameworks used to study order in living organisms. These works are relevant to understanding the scientific method in ecology and the structural basis of evolution, distinct from creationist arguments.
