TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Systems science

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/systems_science.html

Systems science refers to the interdisciplinary study of complex systems, emphasizing holistic analysis over reductionism. In this context, the bibliography focuses on its application to ecology and environmental modeling, particularly through the work of Bernard C. Patten and colleagues. These sources provide foundational frameworks for understanding ecosystem dynamics, which are relevant to discussions of earth history and biological complexity within the origins and evolution debate.
