TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Tendaguru

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/tendaguru.html

Tendaguru refers to a Late Jurassic fossil-bearing formation in Tanzania, renowned for its rich dinosaur assemblages. This bibliography collects scientific literature on the geology, paleontology, and history of expeditions to this site. It serves as a resource for understanding deep time and vertebrate evolution, while also addressing historical contexts relevant to the origins and earth history discourse.
