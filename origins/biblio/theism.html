TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Theism

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/theism.html

Theism refers to the belief in a personal deity, a concept central to many creationist arguments and the broader creation/evolution controversy. This bibliography collects sources examining the intersection of religious belief with origins, science, and earth history. It includes works on the design argument, the historical relationship between science and religion, and critiques of creationism from both secular and religious perspectives.
