Theoretical biology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/theoretical_biology.html

Theoretical biology explores the mathematical and conceptual frameworks underlying evolutionary processes, complexity, and development. This bibliography collects sources addressing how theoretical models inform our understanding of life’s origins and deep time. It serves as a reference for examining the scientific method in evolutionary theory and countering creationist misinterpretations of biological complexity.
