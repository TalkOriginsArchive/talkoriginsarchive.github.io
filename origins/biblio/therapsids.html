TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Therapsids

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/therapsids.html

Therapsids, often called mammal-like reptiles, are a key group in the evolutionary transition from early synapsids to mammals. This bibliography collects scientific literature on their anatomy, classification, and role in deep time, alongside historical context from the creation/evolution controversy. It serves as a resource for understanding the evidence for gradual evolutionary change and the scientific method in paleontology.
