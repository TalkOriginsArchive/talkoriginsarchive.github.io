Theria

Bibliography index Â· /origins/biblio/theria.html

Theria refers to the infraclass of mammals that includes placentals and marsupials, a key group in understanding vertebrate evolution and deep time. This bibliography primarily cites historical works by Karl Ernst von Baer on comparative embryology, which laid foundational concepts for developmental biology. These sources are relevant to the history of science and the study of origins, illustrating early 19th-century approaches to understanding the development of higher animals.
