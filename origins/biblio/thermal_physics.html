Thermal physics

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/thermal_physics.html

Thermal physics provides the fundamental thermodynamic principles governing energy transfer and entropy, which are essential for understanding deep time, stellar evolution, and the Earth's geological history. This bibliography collects sources on these physical laws, which are often invoked in the creation/evolution controversy to address claims regarding heat flow and the age of the Earth. The citations include both foundational textbooks and specific applications relevant to origins science.
