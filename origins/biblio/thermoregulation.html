Thermoregulation

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/thermoregulation.html

Thermoregulation refers to the biological processes by which organisms maintain their internal body temperatures. This bibliography collects sources on physiological mechanisms, evolutionary adaptations, and ecological implications, including historical debates on dinosaur metabolism. It serves as a reference for understanding how thermal regulation influences evolution, deep time ecology, and the scientific study of life history.
