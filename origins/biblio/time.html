TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Time

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/time.html

Time encompasses the conceptual, physical, and historical dimensions of temporal measurement and perception. This bibliography includes sources on the history of timekeeping, the philosophical "arrows of time," and the scientific understanding of deep time. It also addresses historical controversies, such as Velikovsky’s catastrophism, which challenged established chronological frameworks. These citations explore how time is understood in science, history, and the creation/evolution debate.
