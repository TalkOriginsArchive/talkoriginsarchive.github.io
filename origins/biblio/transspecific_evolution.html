TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Transspecific evolution

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/transspecific_evolution.html

Transspecific evolution refers to evolutionary changes occurring above the species level, often termed macroevolution. This bibliography collects sources discussing the mechanisms and evidence for large-scale evolutionary patterns, such as the origin of new taxa and major transitions in life history. It addresses scientific inquiries into deep time and earth history, distinguishing established biological consensus from creationist claims that deny such processes.
