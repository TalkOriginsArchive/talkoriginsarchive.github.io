TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Tropics

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/tropics.html

Tropics refers to the equatorial regions characterized by distinct climate and biodiversity patterns. This bibliography includes sources on tropical ecology, vegetation classification, and evolutionary biology, such as Dobzhansky’s work on evolution in the tropics. These citations support discussions on earth history, deep time, and the scientific study of biological diversity in high-radiation environments.
