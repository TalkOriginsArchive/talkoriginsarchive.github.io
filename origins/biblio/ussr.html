TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Ussr

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/ussr.html

Ussr references the former Soviet Union, primarily in the context of geological and paleontological research cited in this bibliography. The collection includes studies on Phanerozoic marine inundation patterns and fossil records, reflecting historical scientific contributions from the region. These sources are relevant to understanding deep time, earth history, and secular changes in sedimentary basins, rather than addressing creationist claims or modern political discourse.
