TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Variation

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/variation.html

Variation refers to the heritable differences among individuals that provide the raw material for natural selection and evolutionary change. This bibliography collects sources on the genetic, developmental, and ecological origins of such variation, drawing from foundational texts in evolutionary biology. It addresses how variation arises and is maintained, supporting the scientific understanding of evolution against creationist claims that deny the significance of genetic diversity.
