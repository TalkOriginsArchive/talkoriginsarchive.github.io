TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Varved sediments

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/varved_sediments.html

Varved sediments are annually layered deposits that provide high-resolution records of past environmental conditions and geological processes. This bibliography collects sources on their formation, dating, and use in reconstructing climate history, supporting scientific understandings of deep time. It also addresses how these features are sometimes cited in creationist arguments regarding rapid deposition, clarifying the distinction between natural varve formation and flood-based models.
