TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Virology

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/virology.html

Virology is the scientific study of viruses, their structure, classification, and interactions with host organisms. This bibliography includes foundational texts and research on viral evolution, such as the diversity of the AIDS virus, which illustrates rapid genetic change and adaptation. These sources provide evidence for evolutionary processes and help clarify misconceptions often raised in creationist arguments regarding the origins of biological complexity.
