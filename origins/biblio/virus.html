TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Virus

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/virus.html

Virus collects sources on viral biology, molecular virology, and specific pathogen studies. While primarily focused on medical and biological research, these citations provide context for understanding viral evolution, origins of life debates, and the scientific method in addressing disease. The collection includes foundational texts and recent molecular analyses of various viruses.
