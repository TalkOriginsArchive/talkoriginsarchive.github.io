TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Vision

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/vision.html

Vision covers the biological mechanisms of photoreception and visual system evolution across diverse taxa. This bibliography includes scientific literature on the adaptation and phylogenetic history of eyes, relevant to understanding complex biological structures. It addresses how visual capabilities evolved, providing evidence for deep time and evolutionary processes rather than supporting creationist claims.
