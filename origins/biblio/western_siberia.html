TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Western siberia

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/western_siberia.html

Western siberia refers to a major geological province in Russia, primarily studied for its vast oil and gas reserves and complex stratigraphy. This bibliography collects sources on the region's deep-time geological history, including sedimentary basins and fossil records like Jurassic insects. These citations support understanding of earth history and resource geology, rather than addressing creationist claims or evolutionary controversies directly.
