TalkOrigins Bibliography Topic

Yellowstone

Bibliography index · /origins/biblio/yellowstone.html

Yellowstone serves as a critical natural laboratory for understanding deep time, volcanic processes, and the geological history of North America. This bibliography collects sources ranging from foundational 19th-century surveys to modern scientific studies, illustrating how the region’s unique geology informs broader debates on earth history and scientific methodology. It provides context for discussions on natural phenomena often cited in creation/evolution controversies.
