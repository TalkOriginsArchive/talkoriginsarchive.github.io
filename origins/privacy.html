Privacy Policy
  • This site does not sell any information of any sort to any third party whatsoever nor does it accept any paid advertisements.
  • All cookies that are set by this site expire at the end of the session. The vast majority of pages in this site do not set cookies.
  • This site will not use a provided email address to subscribe you to a mailing list.
  • No email address provided will be shared with anyone besides site administrators, volunteers, or authors with the exception of the feedback section which the user has the option of having his email address posted with his name. Feedback users who want their feedback publicly posted but do not want their email address posted as well should not provide their email address. Those who do choose to publicly list their email address with their feedback should be aware that third parties beyond the control of this site can collect them for their own purposes.
  • Those using the chat section are warned not to give out any information in chat that they would not wished to be generally known. The chat applet is ran by VolanoChat and is beyond the control of this site. Be sure to read its legal notice (scroll to "Communications with this Site") for its policies.
  • This site is the work of unpaid volunteers. The site can be contacted in several ways. 1) It maintains a contact page. 2) Many signed pieces are linked to contact information for the author. 3) Many volunteers and users of this site read the talk.origins Usenet newsgroup. (The newsgroup is a separate entity from this Archive.)
  • Also see the welcome page for information of what this Archive is about and how to access the talk.origins newsgroup.
