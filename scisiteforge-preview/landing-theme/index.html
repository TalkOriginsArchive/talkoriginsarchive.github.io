Talk.origins is a Usenet newsgroup devoted to the discussion and debate of biological and physical origins. Most discussions in the newsgroup center on the creation/evolution controversy, but other topics of discussion include the origin of life, geology, biology, catastrophism, cosmology, and theology.

The TalkOrigins Archive is a collection of articles and essays, most of which appeared in talk.origins at one time or another. The archive provides mainstream scientific responses to frequently asked questions and recurring creationist claims.

The archive also collects bibliographies, discussion resources, feedback, post archives, administration notes, awards, and background material for readers who want to follow the details beyond short answers.
